# Privacy Policy — Aki In Time

Last Updated: March 15, 2026


## Overview

Aki In Time ("Aki") is designed with privacy in mind.

The app does not collect personal information.  
All product data is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.


## Data Storage

Information you add to Aki is stored locally on your device, including:

- Product name
- Expiry date
- Quantity
- Barcode (if available)

This data is **not transmitted to any server operated by the developer**.

If you delete the app, this data is removed from your device.


## Barcode Lookup

When you scan a product barcode, Aki may query the **OpenFoodFacts** database to identify the product.

Only the barcode number is sent.

The following information is **not shared**:

- your name
- your location
- any personal identifiers

OpenFoodFacts is an open food database operated by a non-profit organization.

OpenFoodFacts Privacy Policy:  
https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy


## Camera Usage

Aki uses the device camera to:

- scan product barcodes
- recognize expiry dates

All image processing happens **locally on your device**.  
Images are not stored or transmitted.


## Notifications

Aki can send **local notifications** to remind you when a product is approaching its expiry date.

These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent through any external server.


## Data Sharing

Aki does **not**:

- collect personal data
- track users
- use analytics services
- use advertising networks
- sell or share user data

The updated version will always be available at this page.
