## How it works

### Adding a product

1. Tap **+ Add**  
2. Scan the barcode → Product found automatically  
3. Point your camera at the expiry date → Recognized instantly  
4. Adjust quantity if needed  
5. Tap **Save**

### Managing products

- Tap a product to edit name, date, or quantity  
- Swipe left to delete  
- Long press to delete with confirmation  
- Use search to quickly find products

## Notifications

Aki sends a quiet notification **3 days before a product expires**.

To enable notifications:

**Settings → Notifications → Aki**


---

## Troubleshooting

### Barcode not scanning

- Make sure camera permission is enabled  
- Try better lighting  
- Some products may not exist in the OpenFoodFacts database  
- Use **Enter name manually** if needed


### Date not recognized

- Point the camera clearly at the expiry date  
- Try different angles or lighting  
- Use **Enter date manually** if recognition fails


### Days remaining not updating

Days refresh when the app is opened again.  
If the value looks incorrect, close and reopen the app.


### Notifications not working

Check that notifications are enabled:

**Settings → Notifications → Aki**

Notifications appear only **3 days before expiry**.

## Privacy

Aki stores all data **locally on your device**.

The only external request is:

- Barcode lookup to **OpenFoodFacts.org** (open food database)

Aki collects **no personal data**.

- No tracking  
- No ads
